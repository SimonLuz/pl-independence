That was the world of only black 'n' white

This world was black and white. These were the only two colors that existed for the better part of this century. These short years that we were given between 1918 and Nazi German attack in 1939 gave testimony to the hunger/resilience of people who had been waiting for almost 5 generations to get their country and their freedom back. Guns and soldiers boots were heavy-black, smiles of ordinary people were grayish, sometimes white. People raised on the same books and having familiar names could turn out spies or agents. Troubles was our daily bread. but the daily bread was hard to come by. The bottom line is that in that system everything is hard - from trusting your neighbor, through buing a toilet paper , the system is designed to make everything a toil difficult, hard.
